Stable diffusion

Stable Diffusion: A deep learning model released in 2022, utilizing diffusion techniques. It's part of the AI boom and excels at generating detailed images based on text descriptions. Developed by researchers from Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich and Runway, it's used for tasks like image generation guided by text prompts, inpainting, and outpainting.
